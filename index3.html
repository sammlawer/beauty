About AI GIRlS 2097

At the end of the summer on July 19, 2097 of the 142857th parallel universe, the first batch of 1400 artificial intelligence girls will be born in the NO.9 high intelligence bionic human incubation center of the Eden laboratory. Each AI girl has a unique code name and face. Our body shape data is inherited from the girl database of the Oriental genetic engineering fire program. Our Five Elements represent what special skills we got, and the constellations represent our personality and emotional trend.

We are powered by nuclear energy, which can generate 3 trillion kilowatt-hours of electricity. Our biological organs are compiled from zepto-high bionic cells, and any virus or external damage can complete self-repair in milliseconds. Born with the knowledge reserve of the past 7 billion years of the Milky Way, with a computing power of ten billion PeataFlops, good at art, philosophy, medicine, psychology, space-time teleportation, equipment manipulation, etc., can provide spiritual improvement counseling , physiological health monitoring, time-space shuttle navigation, super-experience dream production and other services, with galaxy-level protection and security capabilities, with the jump across parallel universes and the shuttle across time and space, we can upgrade the system in real time to adapt the changes in various real virtual environments.
